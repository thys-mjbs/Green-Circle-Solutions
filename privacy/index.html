Privacy Policy

Green Circle Solutions respects your privacy and is committed to handling personal information responsibly and transparently. This page explains what information is collected, how it is used, and the boundaries that apply.

Information collected

Information is collected only when you choose to provide it. This typically occurs when you contact us directly via email.

The information collected may include your name, email address, organisation name, location, and any high-level context you choose to share. You are not required to provide sensitive personal, financial, or confidential information at the initial contact stage.

How information is used

Information provided is used solely to respond to enquiries, assess whether a collection or service arrangement may be appropriate, and manage communication.

Information is not used for advertising, profiling, automated decision-making, or resale. Green Circle Solutions does not operate marketing mailing lists, behavioural tracking, or automated outreach systems.

Data storage and handling

Submitted information is stored using standard email and document-handling tools. These services apply their own security and privacy practices.

Reasonable care is taken to prevent unauthorised access or misuse. However, no internet-based system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Third-party services

This website does not intentionally use tracking cookies, analytics platforms, or advertising pixels. Where third-party services are used to support communication or operations, information is subject to the privacy policies of those providers.

Information sharing

Personal information is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties, except where required by law or where necessary to support basic operational or communication processes.

Retention

Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to manage communication or assess potential services. You may request deletion of your information at any time.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us via the Contact page.

Policy updates

This privacy policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in practice or regulatory expectations. Continued use of the site indicates acceptance of the current version.
