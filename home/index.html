Reliable recycling collection
for SMEs, light commercial & residential complexes

Green Circle Solutions provides practical recycling collection for small to mid-sized businesses and households. We collect agreed recyclable materials on a consistent schedule and remove them efficiently from your premises.

Where municipal services are irregular or insufficient, we provide a dependable alternative. Collections are structured around real operating conditions, not ideal assumptions.

The objective is simple: recyclable material should not accumulate, lose value, or end up in landfill due to avoidable friction.

Get in touch What we do

What we do

We provide durable collection bags and agree upfront which recyclable materials will be accepted and how they should be separated.

You fill the bags as part of your normal routine. We determine a suitable collection frequency based on your volume and space constraints.

How it works

Collections take place at agreed intervals. If volumes increase or operational needs change, arrangements can be adjusted.

Materials are collected and directed into established recycling channels through structured handling and transport.

Who it is for

This service is suited to small and mid-sized businesses, property owners, and households that generate recyclable material consistently and require a dependable removal arrangement.
